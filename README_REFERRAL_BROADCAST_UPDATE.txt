Referral Dashboard + Media Broadcast Update

Added:
1. Referral Dashboard
   - /referral and /referrals show a dashboard.
   - Shows referral link, total referrals, activated premium referrals, pending referrals, premium days earned, premium expiry, and recent referral status.
   - Referral button label changed to Referral Dashboard.

2. Automatic Premium Referral Reward
   - When a referred user activates paid Premium through M-Pesa callback, the referrer automatically receives 1 extra premium day.
   - Reward is given only once per referred user.

3. Broadcast Upgrade
   - /broadcast your message still sends normal text broadcast.
   - Reply /broadcast to any Telegram message to copy/broadcast it to all users.
   - Supports photos, videos, documents/files, voice/audio, stickers, captions, and text.

How to broadcast media:
- Send or forward the video/photo/file to the bot chat.
- Reply to that message with: /broadcast
- The bot copies it to all registered users.
